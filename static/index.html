Observer Ward

A DOTA 2 bot for Discord

Commands

Configure

/configure <channelId>

Tell Observer Ward where you want to post game results.

Subscribe Players

/subscribe <dotaID> <nickname>

Tell Observer Ward which players to follow, and what to call them. When followed players finish a DOTA2 game, the results will be posted to the configured channel.

Unsubscribe Players

/unsubscribe <dotaID>

Made a mistake? Not interested anymore? Unsubscribe a from a players updates with this command.

List Players

/list_players

See who the bot is following.
